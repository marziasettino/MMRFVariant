MMRFVariantPackage - Visualize

library(MMRFVariantPackage)

MMRFVariant_PlotbyEffect_Impact

Visualize Impact and Effect of annotated variants.

variant.impact_effect<-MMRFVariant_PlotbyEffect_Impact(variant.ann,topN=50,
                                                       height=10, width=15, 
                                                       filenm="PlotbyEffectImpact")
variant.impact_effect
Resulting plot

ListGene<-c("PRDM16", "AGO1","TNNI3K")
